Forum Theatre
FORUM THEATRE

Most training tells you what to do differently. Forum theatre lets you actually do it.

Professional actors perform a scenario your team will recognise. Then your people stop watching and start directing. They step in, change the approach, and see the impact of each choice play out in real time.

The learning comes from being on your feet, not from a workbook.
