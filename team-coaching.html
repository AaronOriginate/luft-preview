The Problem

Why One Session Won't Fix It

Trust in managers has dropped from 46% to 29%. Teams without trust don't collaborate. They protect themselves, avoid conflict, and eventually leave.

A single workshop can't rebuild that. Trust gets built through repeated honesty, and that takes more than an afternoon.

What Shifts Over Time

People speak up because they know it won't be held against them. Communication gets honest, goals get owned, and purpose becomes the reason people show up.
