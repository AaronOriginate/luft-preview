What Coaching With Me Looks Like

I'm an EMCC-accredited executive coach and a professional actor. When you're preparing for a tough conversation, I don't just talk you through it. I play the other person. We rehearse it live until you know exactly how you want to show up.

Everything we discuss stays between us. That confidentiality is what makes the space safe enough to be genuinely open.
